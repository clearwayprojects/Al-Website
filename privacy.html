Welcome to Play-by-Play Sportscast Training ("we," "us," or "our").
Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.playbyplaysportscasttraining.com (the “Site”), make a purchase via PayPal, or contact us through our contact form.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information you provide:
    • When making a purchase: Name, email address, shipping address, payment details (handled securely by PayPal)
    • When using our contact form: Name, email address, and any information you include in your message
  • Automatically Collected Information:
    • Device and usage data, such as IP address, browser type, and usage patterns, through cookies or similar technologies (if used)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill your orders (via PayPal)
  • Respond to your inquiries and messages
  • Maintain the security and functionality of the Site
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information.

  • PayPal: For processing your payments. Your payment information is handled directly by PayPal according to their Privacy Statement.
  • Service Providers: Who help us operate our Site and deliver our services.
  • Legal Authorities: As required by law, regulation, or court order.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. You may adjust your browser settings to disable cookies, which may affect site functionality.

5. Your Rights (California Consumer Privacy Act "CCPA")

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Request disclosure of the personal information we collect, use, or disclose
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your information)
  • Not receive discriminatory treatment if you exercise any of your CCPA rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us at pbpst@playbyplaysportscasttraining.com.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, but no Internet transmission is 100% secure.

7. Children and Students’ Privacy

Our site is intended for students and individuals interested in sportscasting, broadcasting, and related training. While we welcome high school and college-age users, our services are not directed at or intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If we discover we have inadvertently gathered such data, we will promptly delete it.

If you are a parent or guardian and suspect your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can address the issue.

Notice for Student Users:

If you are a student under the age of 18, please discuss using this website and submitting personal information (such as via the contact form or purchasing) with your parent, guardian, or teacher.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with a new effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or wish to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us.
