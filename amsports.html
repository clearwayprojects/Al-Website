Welcome to AlanMark Sports Productions

A Digital/Video Internet Sportscasting Company

Your school or conference will now have the opportunity to reach alumni, families, boosters, administrators, students, fans, and advertisers with live digital video streaming of all home sporting events

The Founders


AlanMark Sports Productions presents an internet sports play-by-play company - Your complete digital/video broadcast and play-by-play production for prep and college sporting events.

This is your own internet broadcast network.

This helps to promote, market, and create interest in the school and conference to alumni, boosters, sponsors, advertisers, students, parents, community, and fans.

Advertising - Sponsors would advertise in the game program, the school's website, and game broadcasts. School keeps 100% of advertising revenue.

Promotions and Marketing - Schools can sell advertising, promote fund raising and donations, acknowledge boosters, alumni, and advertisers, while promoting upcoming events (general, academic, and sports).

Since 2003, AlanMark Sports will broadcast and produce approximately 100 athletic events on the internet each year.
